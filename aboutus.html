About Us

Experience life with color

The Beginning

Years ago, the creators of Kolore struggled with pre-mature greying, lifeless and dry hair. Then they decided to create a consumer-centered product that would allow millions of people around the world struggling with similar problems to overcome them. The founders of Kolore want to revive your hair and give them a new lease on life!

Our Mission

We want to bring out the hidden inner beauty and lustre of your hair by providing special care and attention to it! We use natural and organic ingredients, which provide deep nourishment to your hair and restore its health. Kolore will give you a professional hair make-over in a bottle!
